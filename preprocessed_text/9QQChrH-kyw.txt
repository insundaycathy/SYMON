graduation day arrived, a group of high school students celebrated it by staying three nights in the wet valley water park for fun. lots of parties and youth excitement there. but one night, a mysterious man secretly placed two iron pieces in the middle of one of the water slides, and a terrible thing started to happen. the teenagers held a race on the third day where each student raced to slide to be the fastest. but the teenagers were not aware that their bodies would be mutilated in the middle of the tube slide. the visitors in the swimming pool began to scream hysterically when they saw the bodies of the victims. the pool had now turned blood red, and the fun day turned into a disaster. however, the culprit had slipped through the crowd undetected. how can this mystery be solved? what is the real motive of the perpetrator?
